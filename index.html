About me

I'm an AI developer and automation engineer with a focus on building practical, production-ready systems — not demos. Most of my work sits at the intersection of LLMs, data engineering, and real-world business workflows.

My projects span hospitality, fashion tech, fisheries analytics, and maritime intelligence. What they share: a need to turn messy, domain-specific data into something useful for the people actually doing the work.

I prefer depth over breadth — understanding a domain well enough to build something that actually replaces manual effort, not just assists with it.

Outside of engineering, I'm drawn to problems where good data is hidden in plain sight — PDFs, scanned forms, scattered APIs — and automation can unlock it.
